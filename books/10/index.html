Remind yourself#

© Joel Aguilozi, 2020

What is immortality? And why do many desire it so fervently? I sat down and I would have liked to have a cup of tea, because I need some time to tell you my story, they told me It tastes “very delicious” it’s a shame but I never could nor will I be able to taste it. Have you ever imagined a world without feelings? Well if you never did or I welcome mine.

Well, let me tell you, I experience a world without feelings, it seems strange to me, not knowing anything, not feeling anything, although I also have my qualities and I am immortal. Will you ask yourself why I want to love, hate, if eternal life? is he on my side? Good because I’m about to go crazy.

Perfection is also part of me, I never make mistakes and I rarely do. I’ve read millions of novels, and I never got excited even if I tried. What a shame that I only remember the teachings of such good authors. I also watched millions of movies and none of them brought a tear to my eye, I always ask myself, What is feeling? By

Now no one knew how to explain it to me.

That’s why I always tell my colleagues (who can feel) take advantage, don’t be afraid to laugh, to get emotional, to cry, and live; Do not fear death, because it also part of life. They tell me “you say so because you don’t have to worry about it.” How sad, if they had to live what I live, I think they would last a week at most.

In my search I found that eternity is linked to memories, memory, and feeling, in other words to living each day as if it were the last. But I know that every being in this life has a purpose. Dear reader, I am in a position to tell you that I am a robot, created by human beings, despite having intelligence, reason did not give me the opportunity to feel.

I’m just going to ask you something, now that you’ve read part of my story, do me a favor; Live your life to the fullest, don’t think about death, give value to the present, and strive for the future. Because as a human being you have a great gift, and great qualities. So great that you can even be immortal, do you know how? Through your actions guided by your feelings and your dreams, actions that will make you a better person, perform a great feat and become an immortal being, be remembered and captured in memory for all eternity!
