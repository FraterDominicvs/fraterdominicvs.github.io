The beginning#

© Joel Aguilozi, 2020

For a while I decide to leave everything as it is; I need to assimilate these changes. I don’t move, I just wait for the moment!… Wait a minute… I don’t know what to expect! I am recovering my original form. Something creaks and creaks again. From time to time I shudder. There is a part of me that seems to cry; However, I feel fine, although a little dazed. I try not to think, I stay very, very still and make out millions of particles dancing around me. And so the first organisms appear, tiny but strong, with the desire to live; It’s just a matter of time. Finally they evolve and when everything is ready to receive him, he appears. It is a being with Machine Translated by Google

I asked whoever sent me to do this task: values! and he granted them to me, so I distributed them among those who seemed weaker to me; those, whom I called “humans”, and whose survival instincts are almost null, and their intelligence leaves much to be desired. On the other hand, the others are capable of taking care of themselves and searching for food. Now everything depends on those “humans” that live on my surface. They forbade me to intervene, yes, I warned them not to mistreat me, if that happens, the consequences will be fatal.

I grew and grew, each thing had its name and they called me “Mother Earth.”
