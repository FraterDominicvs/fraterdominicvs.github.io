About me

    2 min · Joel Aguilozi | Translations:
    • Español

    I’m Joel Aguilozi, a Science Fiction writer eager to expand my horizons and venture into the world of production and artistic direction. As a passionate art enthusiast, I have worked as a chronicler and photographer at music concerts, and I am very dedicated to video games as a fan, gamer, tester, and analyst.

    I have always enjoyed creating things, and to my writing production, I add experiences in programming and 3D design. Since completing my studies, I have worked in various IT sectors, including moderation and analysis, testing multiplatform games, and venturing into production.

    Since finishing my studies, I have worked in various IT sectors, such as moderation and analysis, testing cross-platform games, and venturing into production.

    My strengths are creating storylines, designing timelines, and providing user feedback. The last few years in the gaming sector have been where I have grown the most personally and professionally, facing all kinds of challenges and meeting exceptional people.

    I consider myself a lifelong learner, always thirsty for knowledge and eager to explore new technologies.

    Creating an immersive and disruptive experience with deep emotional content is not only one of my goals but also part of my growth and evolution.

    I do sports to disconnect and recharge, as well as spend time with my family. At night, I usually continue studying to delve deeper into this wonderful world of technology. There is always something new to learn, whether it’s reading books, watching videos, taking courses, or writing notes. Besides that, the best way to learn is by doing, not just watching others do it. Therefore, I dedicate time to creating projects or proof of concept on topics I want to delve into and master to later apply them in my work or future projects. You can see some of my projects at Projects.
